ABOUT ME

Hello there and welcome to my page! I am brand new to this massive coding world and hope to join many others in becoming a developer.

First, let's get to know who I am: Currently, I work as an operations coordinator at an e-commerce retail company. The bulk of my duties include day-to-day data entry tasks, analysis, building and running reports for my operations team. I am also a Cal State Fullerton alumi, from the class of 2018 with a Bachelors in Human Communication Studies and an emphasis on Interpersonal Communication. On my off-time, you would typically find me at the gym powerlifting or weightlifting. Fitness is my life and it's pretty much what I like to do for "fun". Aside from that, I also enjoy spending time outside to remind myself there's still a world out there that awaits me.

One of the reasons why I want to delve into this industry is because I recently realized how much I found myself enjoying styling the content of a blog (most commonly MySpace and Tumblr back in the old days--never thought I'd be saying that now). My peers that have already succeeded in attaining a career in development also motivated me to give this coding journey a shot, purely based off how happy they have been. They have been very open and willing to persuade me to tackle coding, all while reminding me about my potential.

This is only square one, but let's hope I can ride this out to the end and beyond!
